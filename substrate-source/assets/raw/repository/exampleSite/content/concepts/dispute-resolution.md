Disputes are knowledge, not cleanup errors.

[XanaNode](/node/xananode) preserves disagreement about claims, relationships, identity mappings, and merge decisions. The contested state remains traceable through [provenance](/node/provenance), evidence, counterevidence, and explicit relationship types such as `contradicts`, `disputes`, `possible_match`, and `disputed_identity`.

The goal is navigable uncertainty, not instant consensus.
