Deep linking points at an addressable part of a node rather than only at a whole page.

In [XanaNode](/node/xananode), stable fragment ids and tumbler addresses give those links protocol identity. A deep link can identify the source node, version, and fragment being discussed or transcluded.

That is the practical bridge between ordinary hyperlinks and the [provenance](/node/provenance)-preserving reuse imagined by Xanadu.
