In 1945, Vannevar Bush worried that humanity was producing more knowledge than it could meaningfully navigate.

{{< claim id="info-not-knowledge" >}}
Information is not knowledge. Knowledge lives in relationships, context, provenance, contradiction, and memory.
{{< /claim >}}

The web solved publication. It did not solve memory.

{{< transclude source="bush-as-we-may-think" label="Bush on information overload" >}}

That is the thing AI is exposing now.
