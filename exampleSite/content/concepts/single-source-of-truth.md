Single-source-of-truth systems are useful when consensus, control, and simplicity matter.

They become fragile when knowledge is disputed, partial, culturally specific, politically sensitive, or evolving.

[XanaNode](/node/xananode) favors interoperable sources of context over one enforced source of truth.
