A normal hyperlink says that two things are connected.

A typed relationship says how they are connected.

That difference matters. `supports`, `contradicts`, `explains`, `derived_from`, `memorializes`, `transcludes`, and `deep_links_to` carry different meanings and should not be flattened into generic relatedness.
