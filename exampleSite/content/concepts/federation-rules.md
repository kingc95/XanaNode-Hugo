Federation starts from substrate sovereignty.

Another substrate may import, map, compare, or reject data, but it must not silently overwrite [provenance](/node/provenance), collapse disagreement, or erase local moderation decisions.

The canonical rule is simple: merge by adding structure, not by destroying context.
