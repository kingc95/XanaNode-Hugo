---
title: "Unlabeled Hyperlink"
type: "concept"
importance: 3
summary: "A link that connects two resources without preserving why the connection matters."
relationships:
  - type: "threatens"
    target: "provenance"
---

The web hyperlink is powerful because it is simple. It spread because it was easy.

Its weakness is that it usually does not say whether the target supports, contradicts, defines, documents, quotes, explains, or merely mentions the source.
