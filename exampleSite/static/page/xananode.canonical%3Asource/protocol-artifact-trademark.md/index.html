# XanaNode Trademark Policy

The XanaNode name, XanaNode logo, and related brand assets identify the canonical XanaNode protocol and project.

You may use the name XanaNode to make accurate factual statements, such as:

  • "implements the XanaNode protocol"
  • "XanaNode-compatible"
  • "built with XanaNode schemas"
  • "uses XanaNode Core"

You may not use the XanaNode name or logo in a way that implies endorsement, official status, or control by the XanaNode project unless that relationship is true.

Implementations should identify themselves as independently authored unless they are maintained by the XanaNode project. For example:

ExampleApp is an independent XanaNode-compatible implementation.

Modified logos, confusingly similar marks, and product names that imply ownership of XanaNode itself are not permitted without permission.
